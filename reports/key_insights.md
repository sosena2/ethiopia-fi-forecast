## 1. Access has stagnated despite mobile money expansion
Account ownership grew only +3pp (46% → 49%) between 2021–2024, the smallest
gain of any survey wave, despite Telebirr and M-Pesa combined onboarding
tens of millions of registered mobile money users in the same window.

## 2. Registered ≠ active
There is a large gap between registered mobile money accounts (Telebirr:
54M+ users) and the active-usage rate captured in Findex-style surveys —
suggesting substantial account dormancy inflates headline registration figures.
